Churn → Every player who churns on Day 1 never reaches L5. Fixing churn unlocks 66% more potential shoppers.
Invisible Shop → Even retained players can't spend if the store is hidden. Personalisation doesn't help if the purchase gate is locked. Fixing placement unlocks the entire revenue funnel.
No Personalisation → Without segmentation, pricing is blind, pushes are noise, and retention mechanics can't target. Personalisation is the multiplier on both churn and conversion fixes.
